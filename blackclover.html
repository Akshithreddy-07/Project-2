The World of Magic

In a world where magic is everything, Asta and Yuno are two orphans who were abandoned at a church on the same day. While Yuno possesses exceptional magical power and the legendary four-leaf grimoire, Asta is the only person in the world without a single drop of mana.

Produced by Studio Pierrot, the anime premiered in 2017 and quickly became a staple of the Shonen genre, known for its intense battles, underdog themes, and the chaotic yet lovable members of the Black Bulls squad.

The Journey to Wizard King

The story follows Asta's journey as he joins the Black Bulls, the most destructive and disorderly Magic Knight squad in the Clover Kingdom. Despite his lack of magic, Asta acquires a mysterious five-leaf grimoire that grants him the power of Anti-Magic.

  • The Rivalry: Asta and Yuno race to see who will become the next Wizard King.
  • The Threat: Together with his teammates, Asta must defend the kingdom from the Eye of the Midnight Sun, devils, and warring nations.
  • The Bond: Under Captain Yami Sukehiro, the Black Bulls learn to surpass their limits in the face of despair.

The Manga Source

Created by Yūki Tabata, the Black Clover manga began serialization in Weekly Shōnen Jump in February 2015.

The manga is praised for its incredibly fast-paced storytelling and detailed artwork, particularly during the Spade Kingdom Raid arc. In 2023, the manga moved to Jump GIGA to allow the author more time to craft the final climactic battles of the series.

Status: Ongoing / Final Arc
