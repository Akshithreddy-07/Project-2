The Tale of the Uzumaki Naruto

Twelve years before the start of the series, the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox attacked the Hidden Leaf Village. To save the people, the Fourth Hokage sealed the beast inside a newborn infant named Naruto Uzumaki.

Produced by Studio Pierrot, the anime is divided into two parts: Naruto, following his childhood adventures, and Naruto: Shippuden, covering his teenage years and his quest to save his friend Sasuke.

The Path of the Ninja

Ostracized by his village, Naruto dreams of becoming the Hokage—the strongest ninja and leader of the village—to earn the respect of everyone who looked down on him.

  • Team 7: Naruto joins Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno under the guidance of Kakashi Hatake.
  • The Akatsuki: A rogue ninja organization seeks to capture all Tailed Beasts to rule the world.
  • Ninjutsu: The series features complex battle systems involving Chakra, hand signs, and legendary techniques like the Rasengan.

Masashi Kishimoto's Legacy

Created by Masashi Kishimoto, the manga debuted in Weekly Shōnen Jump in 1999 and ran for 15 years. It is one of the "Big Three" shonen manga that defined a generation.

The series is beloved for its deep character development, themes of loneliness and redemption, and some of the most iconic fight choreography in manga history.

Status: Completed (Followed by the sequel Boruto).
